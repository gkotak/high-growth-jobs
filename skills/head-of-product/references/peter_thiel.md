# Peter Thiel’s 7 Questions (Reference)

Use these questions to stress-test the startup's strategy. A "Monopoly" answers YES to most.

1.  **The Engineering Question:** Can you create breakthrough technology instead of incremental improvements? (10x better?)
2.  **The Timing Question:** Is now the right time to start your particular business? (Why now?)
3.  **The Monopoly Question:** Are you starting with a big share of a small market? (Niche domination)
4.  **The People Question:** Do you have the right team? (Founder fit)
5.  **The Distribution Question:** Do you have a way to not just create but deliver your product? (Sales strategy)
6.  **The Durability Question:** Will your market position be defensible 10 and 20 years into the future? (Moat)
7.  **The Secret Question:** Have you identified a unique opportunity that others don't see? (Contrarian truth)
